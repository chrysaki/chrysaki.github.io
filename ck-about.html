storytime

For the last 5 years I’m working on the UX/UI realm, and currently, I’m leading the design process for my scrum team. However, my chosen path in Design began much earlier.

Drawing board, inks and pencils
While still in high school I enrolled in an art academy to learn the principles of architectural and still-life pencil drawing. There I got the opportunity to study visual elements, make sense of their structure and experiment with more thorough methods of expressing concepts in the paper.

University years
My studies (BSc in Informatics) were also focused on Design, this time though with an emphasis on human-computer interaction and design and evaluation of user-centered software. This technical background gave me credibility with my engineering colleagues and helped me acquire the necessary problem-solving skills to come up with practical and easy-to-build design solutions.

Early career steps
After graduation, I chose to start my career as a Front-end Developer, driven by the belief that magic in digital products comes from a recipe of beautiful design and elegant code. My training for a hybrid role taught me the tools that are used to give substance to the wireframes and mockups but also how UX Design integrates with agile and scrum.

The magic world of Tech
My challenge is to find ways to combine function and aesthetics in experiences that are shared online. But, to be more effective in my role and lead the design of experiences that have real value for my users, I needed to have a broad idea of the wider topics and issues facing tech teams. My MiM course portfolio (MSc in Management) covered many fascinating business topics, including strategic planning, continuous improvement, project management, customer-centricity & cultivating customer relationships that allowed me to become more versatile as a designer and level up soft skills such as facilitation, negotiation, and persuasion.

We are the craftsmen that implement what users want and companies need. We are almost magicians operating secretly within this astounding digital landscape.
