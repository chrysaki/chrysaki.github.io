storytime

Drawing board, inks and pencils
Drawing was always the means for me to express my thoughts but also make sense of the world around me. So while a student, I enrolled in an art academy in order to learn architectural and still life pencil drawing.

University years
My BS degree in Informatics was focused on design, this time though with an emphasis on design and evaluation of user-centered software, human-computer interaction, interaction design.

Early career steps
When I graduated, I chose to start my career as a Front-end Developer, driven by the belief that magic in digital products comes from a recipe of beautiful design and elegant code. Besides by learning the tools that are used to give substance to the wireframes and mockups, I could level up my design skills. Using the knowledge gained landed me my first hybrid role of a Designer and a Front-End Developer, bridging the design, content and technology teams.

The magic of tech products
Since moving to the UK, my design horizons have been opened up and my own personal challenge is to find ways to combine function and aesthetics in experiences that are shared online.

I'm currently working on the UI/UX realm, helping scrum teams across multiple campaigns.

We are the craftsmen that implement what users want and companies need. We are almost magicians operating secretly within this astounding digital landscape.
