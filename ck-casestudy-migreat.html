Migreat platform

Migreat is the online platform dedicated to democratise visa & local information for migrants before and after the move.

Problem

Over one billion people will migrate over the next five years and will have to navigate through expensive and complex immigration processes. Immigration can be a touchy subject, especially in countries that see a high influx of newcomers, and there is often political pressure to control the flow of immigration to protect resources. Immigrants, however, can make valuable contributions to society.

Approach

Migreat helps migrants work through the proper immigration channels so that they can have the opportunities they need to be fully integrated in their communities.

The team provides expert legal advice and tailored information to suit the needs of every individual: students can gain access to higher education, families can be reunited with their loved ones and each individual can apply to use their skills for gainful employment. For the first time migrants are being offered affordable and effective assistance on complex immigration regulations.

Solution

Migreat takes out the complexity of immigration rules and the opacity of information on visa, jobs and study opportunities across countries. The online platform is very user friendly -- a strong contrast to everything we expect when dealing with all things visa -- and personalised to the user. This is due to the algorithm that creates a customised to-do list of every step in the visa application process, ensuring that the end users get accurate and up-to-date information for each unique situation.

Migreat Visa Finder Application
Application's Link

The team simplified visa bureaucracy by providing a user-friendly interface and navigation for immigration rules. It allows users to quickly find out the steps needed and costs incurred in getting a visa for a particular country and then make an informed decision about which country to move to. They can create their own free visa todo list, connect to a lawyer and get real answers to all their migration related questions from real people on the Migreat platform. Migreat makes it easier to migrate and it finds the simplest way to migrate.

Migreat Communities Application
Communities Link

Migreat.com also provides a trusted community resource made by foreign nationals that want to discover, access and share contacts, places and social tips about their new home. The aim is to create a place where future and existing immigrants to a city can build their new life, social relationships and network online or from a mobile device.
