Overview

Elemental Concept works with clients, from start-ups to multinational organisations, to help them structure and implement a transformation to their business.

In some cases, this is about capitalising on digital initiatives more quickly than competitors to maintain or increase an advantage in the marketplace. In others, it could be to disrupt the market by introducing a new way to engage with the customer.

The Process

No matter the challenge, the team's priority is always keeping the focus on the end-customer and how to better serve their needs, expectations and preferences.

The consultancy journey is a collaborative process. Engaging the key stakeholders within the business through a series of workshops, we draw out the fundamental problems facing the business and ultimately discover and implement the best solution to this problem.

The consultancy journey can be broken down into 3 stages:

  1. Conceptualisation
  2. Product Discovery
  3. Development
